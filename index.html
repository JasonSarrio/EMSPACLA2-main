Our Mission

Unify Louisiana's EMS professionals in advancing a sustainable, accountable, and patient-centered emergency medical system.

EMSPACLA is committed to strengthening emergency medical services by promoting fair reimbursement, opposing anti-competitive system design, and supporting the frontline providers who answer the call.

The goal is simple: give Louisiana's leaders verifiable information about EMS issues and solutions, so every community has access to timely, high-quality emergency care.

What We Do

The voice, power, and protection of EMS professionals

EMSPAC Louisiana stands behind the people who answer the call — across the street, the agency, and the state capitol.

Mutual Aid for EMS Professionals

We've got each other's backs. From on-scene support to departmental challenges, EMSPACLA members stand together and show up when it matters most.

Advocacy

We advocate for the policies, funding, and laws that EMS workers and our communities deserve. Your voice. Our power. Real change.

Organizing for the Future

We build power at every level — on the street, in our agencies, and in our communities — so future generations of EMS workers have a better future than we do today.

What We Stand For

Four commitments that guide our advocacy

01

Fair Reimbursement

EMS must be paid in a way that reflects the lifesaving care it delivers — not treated as an afterthought in the system.

02

Open, Fair Systems

We oppose anti-competitive system design that puts profit ahead of patients and limits a community's options for care.

03

Frontline Support

We stand behind the providers who answer the call — fair pay, sustainable workloads, and a voice in policy.

04

Timely Access

Every community deserves dependable access to high-quality emergency care, whenever and wherever it's needed.
